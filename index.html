How to install a bookmarklet (click me)

Just drag and drop the hyperlinks (underlined blue) onto your bookmarks bar

Dark Mode - Makes your page to dark mode and also changes the font to comic sans.

Dev Console - When you click the link it loads up a dev console. To access it, click on the button in the bottom right of your screen.

Dictionary - Put a word into the prompt and it will give you the definition

History Flooder - Enter the amount of times you want the website you are own to show up in your history

Snake Mods - Mods for the Google Snake Game.

Tab Cloak - Choose what you want to name and also choose the favicon you want with the number it has beside it.

Tab Close Preventer - Prevents your tab from being closed by your Teacher

Youtube Admin Bypass - Bypass your school blocking videos on Youtube

Youtube Ad Skip - Skips the adds on Youtube
